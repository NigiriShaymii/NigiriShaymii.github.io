Artist Statement

I am 21 years old, currently enrolling in San Jose State University with a major of "Concentration in Studio Practice". My dream career is to create games as a video game designer, working on lots of games that'll reflect creativity, story, and imagination. My philosophy is that to create a well designed game is to have four elements: visuals, music, mechanics, and story. Each element is crucial to bring out the entertainment and the engagement to the players and the audience in hopes of influencing and inspiring their creativity.

There are incredible creators like Shigero Miyomoto, Steven Spielberg, Steve Jobs, and such who became inspired by the past and the present experience. People like them also have at least a formula to use as their guideline to make their products polished and perfected. I am still finding my own formula and my inspiration as a music composer and a digital artist in order to translate my skills and abilities to my games.

My main objective is to improve and getting used to the art business so I can be determined and pressured (in a good way) to create my creations without rushing. I am concerned that we are losing what's important not just for our friends, families, and the business but ourselves as well. Even though reality can be confusing, nerve-wreaking, and pressurizing, I want to proceed my dream career so I can get comfortable with what I love and confidentally express my love for creating content packaged with visuals, interactions, and emotions.

Thank you for taking your time to read this and I hope we can chat and negotiate if you want to commission me (coming soon...)!
