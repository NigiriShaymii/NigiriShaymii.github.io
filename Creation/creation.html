Coastal Geometry

2018, Illustrator

I Sense And Am Terrified, Self-Portrait

2020, Digital Art

Every time I look at myself in the mirror, I feel lost. It is like I am gazing at someone I should have been familiar with but something changed. My self-portrait reveals how I am robbed by society, manipulated to follow their rules that I am uncomfortable with. Creating this collage stirs discomfort and fear, seeing how much time passes in my life and how much I am manipulated and watched, physically and virtually.
