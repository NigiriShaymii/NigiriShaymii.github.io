Coastal Geometry

2018, Illustrator

As the pedestrians pass by the coast, the sky grew darker, casting the muted clouds throughout the land. Everything's lifeless and almost empty. Only humans scavenge what is left in the area. I feel the resonance of the soothing dystopia when I took a picture at that sidewalk. Reflecting the scene with vector art gives this haziness like a fog that blankets the memory like a translucent curtain.
